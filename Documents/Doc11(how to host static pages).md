### How to host site in just 4-5 Clicks?

- Make your project in IDE.
- Press on cloud/deploy icon.
- you'll be redirected to auth if you don't have an account either, to the host page.
- slug it (name) of your site.
- upload your projects files or select directly from the IDE storage.
- Set visibility.
- Click Publish.
